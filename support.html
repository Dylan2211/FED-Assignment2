Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list an item for sale?
To list an item, click on 'Sell' at the top of the page, upload images, add a description, and set your price.
Is there a fee for selling items?
No, listing an item is free! However, we may charge a small fee for premium listings that boost visibility.
How can I contact a seller?
You can message the seller directly by clicking on the 'Contact Seller' button on their listing page.
What payment methods are accepted?
Buyers and sellers can agree on payment methods. We recommend cash on delivery, bank transfer, or using secure third-party payment services.
Can I return an item after purchase?
Since this is a second-hand marketplace, all sales are final unless otherwise agreed upon with the seller.
